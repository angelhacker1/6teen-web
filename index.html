Artist Biography

Joseph Takyi Ansah Prah

I am Joseph Takyi Ansah Prah, known as 6TEEN (Sixth Sense of Talented Entertainment Entity Noon). I was born in Winniba, Central Region, Ghana, and raised in Agona Swedru.

Ever since I was a child, I have been in love with vibrant, neon, and expressive colors, exploring any medium I could get my hands on—from color pencils, acrylic paste, and watercolor to cardboard and other materials. I believe that creativity has no limits, and I make it a point to experiment with everything I can.

My art is influenced by my moods, spirituality, and nature, reflecting my inner world and the world around me. I work across abstract, surreal, realistic, experimental, and conceptual styles, blending them into a vibrant and explosive visual language.

Through my work, I want people to reconnect with their inner child, embrace playfulness, and find joy in life’s small and large wonders. My ultimate goal is to be recognized globally, inspiring others to experience creativity, imagination, and freedom without boundaries.

Artist Statement

"I create art to explore the hidden parts of ourselves, to reconnect with the inner child we often suppress in the face of life’s harsh realities. My work is a reflection of emotions, moods, and experiences."

"I work with whatever medium is available... because I believe that art is about expression, not limitation. I mix styles freely to create pieces that are vibrant, explosive, and imaginative."

"I want people to play, dream, and feel joy. My ultimate goal is to inspire others to reconnect with themselves, to feel alive, and to see the world with curiosity and imagination."

Vision & Mission

My Vision

My vision is to help people reconnect with their inner child, to embrace playfulness, curiosity, and joy in life. I aim to create art that inspires freedom, imagination, and happiness, reminding everyone that creativity and wonder are essential to the human spirit.

My Mission

Through my work, I want to leave a positive mark on the world, encouraging people to explore themselves, enjoy life, and celebrate the beauty of childhood memories. My mission is to push artistic boundaries, experiment without limits, and share my vision globally.
